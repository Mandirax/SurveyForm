Survey Form for People affected in chiplun flood


21 July 2021 was the unforgettable day for people of Chiplun city! First time the city experienced the impact of massive floods which was worst than the flood in 2005. Many people lost their homes and shops.Some people lost their lives also. This survey form is made to collect the data of the needs of the flood affected people.Through this form it will become easier for those who want to give a helping hand.
